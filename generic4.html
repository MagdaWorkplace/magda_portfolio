Task 2: Task Analysis

Initial HTA:

0: Select seat(s) and view price

1: Open app and log in

2 Search for film

3 Select cinema and showtime

4 Open seating plan

5 Review availability and prices

6 Choose seat(s)

7 Confirm selection

Timing Notes:

-> Roughly one minute to complete the whole process.

-> The process is straight forward, there are little to no confusions.

-> The option for people with hearing impairments is easy to notice.

-> The only confusion is for seat selection; a change should be made when displaying the price of the seat as it can be a little confusing since the total is right inside it.
